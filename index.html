Retirement Investment Options in India
🏛️
NPS (National Pension)
Market-linked pension. Extra ₹50,000 tax deduction under 80CCD(1B) over the ₹1.5L 80C limit.
Returns: 10-12% p.a.
📘
PPF (Public Provident Fund)
Government-backed, 15-year lock-in. EEE tax status — exempt at all 3 stages. Perfect for conservative allocation.
Returns: ~7.1% p.a.
📈
Equity Mutual Funds
SIP in diversified equity funds for 20-30 year horizon. Best wealth creator. LTCG taxed at 12.5% above ₹1.25L gain.
Returns: 12-15% p.a.
